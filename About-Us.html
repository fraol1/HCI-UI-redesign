About Us

Hi, we are Mark & Angie Gray and 'MGBD Parts' is the part of our business dedicated to keeping Rover P6 cars on the road at cost effective prices. We have over 30 years experience of owning, repairing, restoring and running Rover P6 cars in all variants, 2000, 2200 & 3500, saloon and estate, both as everyday and show condition cars.

Our current fleet includes an everyday user 1966 Rover 2000, and also a 1972 Rover 2000TC export, 1975 Rover 2200 Auto, 1972 Rover 3500 Estoura (estate) and of course, the last P6 off the production line 'VVC 700S', the avocado Rover 3500S. We have had over 25 years involvement in classic car clubs mostly at committee level and have now turned our specialist knowledge and experience into our Rover P6 parts business 'MGBD Parts', which is dedicated to supplying quality parts for all variants of the Rover P6 built between 1963-1977. The parts stocked are predominantly brand new manufactured items but there is also a mix of 'NOS' (New Old Stock), refurbished service exchange and second hand parts.

But if you can't see what you need please email or call us. Also if you need any advice or technical help, we are only a phone call away and we'll be glad to help and what's more, that's free!.
